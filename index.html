How to play

Look through the photo results, and try to guess what Reddit community (or subreddit) the photos are from.

When you have a guess, type it into the guess box and click 'Enter'! If you are close, then you will be shown a hint, if not then you will have to keep guessing.

When you get it right, you will be given points based on your difficulty.

Difficulty & Scoring

Difficulty Description Winning score Skip penalty
Easy Loads the most photos 25 points + time multiplier 5 points
Normal Loads some photos 50 points + time multiplier 10 points
